About the site

This website was created as my first hack for hackBCA III.

I coded this site in html 3 years ago, and decided to give it a new life at the hackathon, aka make it better.

I feel that the Internet is something that connects us all, and thus is also a medium of self-expression. I think that we often forget to code websites in a way that expresses ourselves instead to captivate an audience. We need more sites that are personal.
